About Me

Born in Brisbane and spent majority of my life in Queensland, with a short 5 year stint in Sydney, I have worked in a variety of Industries such as Motor Vehicle Industry, Modelling Agencies, Property Development, Movie Industry, and Hospitality. I have been running my own business for the past 16 years designing and manufacturing airbag suspension for the aftermarket 4wd industry. I started my programming journey about 25 years ago. Having a diverse background in many industries, enables me to cross reference my previous experiences and find a level ground for development and planning. Married with two teenage children, who are both active Football players, most of our free time is spent at football, supporting our club and teams.
